PROJECT SPECIFICATION: EGYPTIAN BANKING APP UI/UX OVERVIEW: Production-grade Egyptian banking mobile application enabling money transfers, bill payments, card payments, and account management. Target: Egyptian citizens/residents. Style: iOS Human Interface Guidelines (clean, minimal, refined). DESIGN SYSTEM: - Primary Blue: #1F5A96 (Egyptian Blue) - Secondary Gold: #D4AF37 (Egyptian Gold) - Success: #34C759, Warning: #FF9500, Error: #FF3B30 - Typography: San Francisco (System Fonts) - Grid: 8px (sm: 8px, md: 12px, lg: 16px) SCREENS TO GENERATE: 1. Splash Screen: Brand intro with bank logo. 2. Login & OTP: Phone number entry (+20) and 6-digit OTP verification. 3. Dashboard: Balance hero card (gradient), quick actions grid, recent transactions. 4. Send Money: 4-step wizard (Recipient, Amount, Review, Success). 5. Bill Payment: Category selection (Electricity, Water, Gas, etc.) and payment flow. 6. Transaction History: Date-grouped list with filtering. 7. Profile & Settings: User info, security toggles, language (AR/EN), and logout. LOCALIZATION: - Support for Arabic (RTL) and English (LTR). - Number formatting: 15,500.75 EGP.
